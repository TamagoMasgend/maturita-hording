## Name 5 international cuisines + example
1. **Italian cuisine:** Known for pasta and pizza. Example: *Spaghetti Carbonara* or *Pizza Margherita*.
2. **Chinese cuisine:** Known for rice, noodles, and stir-fried dishes. Example: *Kung Pao Chicken*.
3. **Mexican cuisine:** Known for spicy flavours, beans, and corn. Example: *Tacos* or *Burritos*.
4. **Japanese cuisine:** Known for raw fish and rice. Example: *Sushi* or *Ramen*.
5. **French cuisine:** Known for elegant dishes, sauces, and pastries. Example: *Ratatouille* or *Croissant*.

## Name 3 typical Czech dishes – describe them
1. **Svíčková na smetaně (Roast sirloin in sour cream sauce):** A very popular traditional dish consisting of beef served in a thick, rich root-vegetable and cream sauce, served with bread dumplings (knedlíky) and topped with a slice of lemon, whipped cream, and cranberry sauce.
2. **Vepřo knedlo zelo (Pork, dumplings, and cabbage):** The national dish of the Czech Republic. It consists of roast pork meat, bread or potato dumplings, and stewed cabbage (which can be sweet or sour).
3. **Smažený sýr (Fried cheese):** A thick slice of cheese (usually Edam) covered in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs, and deep-fried. It is typically served with chips (French fries) and tartar sauce.

## Describe typical English breakfast, lunch and dinner
- **Breakfast:** The traditional "Full English Breakfast" is heavy and cooked. It includes fried eggs, bacon, sausages, baked beans in tomato sauce, grilled tomatoes, mushrooms, and buttered toast, usually accompanied by tea with milk.
- **Lunch:** English lunch is usually a quick, light meal. Often it is just a sandwich, a packet of crisps, or soup, grabbed during a work break.
- **Dinner:** This is the main hot meal of the day, eaten in the evening. A typical dinner could be "meat and two veg" (e.g., roast beef with potatoes and carrots) or popular takeaway meals like Fish and Chips or Chicken Tikka Masala.

## Describe typical Czech breakfast, lunch and dinner
- **Breakfast:** Czech breakfast is usually quick and cold. It consists of bread or a bread roll (rohlík) with butter, cheese, ham, or jam, or perhaps a bowl of cereal with milk. Served with tea or coffee.
- **Lunch:** This is historically the main and warmest meal of the day. It has two courses: a soup (like chicken broth or potato soup) and a main dish (like meat with rice/potatoes/dumplings).
- **Dinner:** Often a lighter meal, occasionally cold (more bread with cold cuts) or a lighter warm dish like pasta or scrambled eggs.

## Recipe of one meal (Pancakes)
**Ingredients:** 2 eggs, 300 ml milk, 150g plain flour, a pinch of salt, oil for frying.
**Instructions:**
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
2. Add the milk and a pinch of salt and whisk together.
3. Gradually add the flour and whisk until the batter is completely smooth and without lumps.
4. Heat a little oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
5. Pour a small amount of batter into the pan and tilt the pan to cover the bottom evenly.
6. Fry for 1-2 minutes until golden, then flip and fry the other side.
7. Serve hot with jam, Nutella, or fresh fruit.
