# 07 - Weather and seasons

## 1. Basic information
The climate of a region depends entirely on its geographical location. The Czech Republic is located in the **mild climate zone** (mírný pás). This means we have a temperate climate with four distinct seasons: Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter. The weather changes gradually, and we do not experience extreme weather events like hurricanes or monsoons.

## 2. Spring
- **Months:** March, April, May.
- **Average temperature:** Around 10°C to 15°C.
- **6 Words for weather:** mild, melting (snow), showery, breezy, unpredictable, clearing.
- **Nature:** Nature wakes up from winter sleep. Snow melts, trees blossom, the first spring flowers appear (snowdrops). Birds return from the south.
- **People activities:** Cleaning gardens, planting seeds, riding bikes, celebrating Easter.

## 3. Summer
- **Months:** June, July, August.
- **Average temperature:** Around 20°C to 30°C.
- **6 Words for weather:** sunny, hot, scorching, humid, thunderstorm, clear (sky).
- **Nature:** Everything is fully green. The sun shines intensely, but occasionally there are heavy summer storms. Crops ripen in the fields.
- **People activities:** Going on holidays to the sea, swimming, sunbathing, barbecuing, camping, picking mushrooms, and hiking.

## 4. Autumn (Fall)
- **Months:** September, October, November.
- **Average temperature:** Around 10°C to 15°C slowly dropping.
- **6 Words for weather:** cloudy, windy, foggy, rainy, gloomy, chilly.
- **Nature:** Leaves on trees change colours (red, yellow, brown) and fall down. Days become shorter, and nights get longer and cooler. Birds fly south.
- **People activities:** Harvesting crops (apples, potatoes), flying kites, raking leaves, starting the new school year.

## 5. Winter
- **Months:** December, January, February.
- **Average temperature:** Around 0°C to -5°C (often freezing).
- **6 Words for weather:** freezing, snowy, icy, frosty, blizzard, overcast.
- **Nature:** Nature is resting. The ground is often covered in snow or ice. Trees are bare without leaves (except evergreens).
- **People activities:** Skiing, snowboarding, ice skating, drinking hot tea or mulled wine, celebrating Christmas and New Year's Eve.

## 6. Clothes to wear in each season
- **Spring:** A light jacket, jeans, sneakers, and long-sleeved t-shirts. Because the weather is unpredictable (April showers), an umbrella is useful.
- **Summer:** Very light clothes: T-shirts, shorts, skirts, dresses, sandals, sunglasses, and a cap to protect from the sun.
- **Autumn:** Waterproof jackets (raincoats), sweaters, trousers, scarves, and waterproof boots.
- **Winter:** Warm and heavy clothes. A thick winter coat, sweaters, winter boots, gloves, a scarf, and a warm hat.

## 7. Clothes for special occasions
- **Final exam (Maturita):** 
  - Boys: A dark suit, a formal shirt, a tie, and dress shoes.
  - Girls: A formal skirt with a blouse or a formal dress (not too short), and elegant shoes (pumps).
- **Ball (Ples):**
  - Boys: A black tuxedo or a formal suit with a bow tie.
  - Girls: A long, elegant evening gown (ball dress), high heels, and jewellery.
- **Job interview:** Smart casual or formal wear depending on the job. A clean shirt, trousers (or a knee-length skirt), looking neat and professional.
- **Sport:** Comfortable activewear. Tracksuits, leggings, a breathable T-shirt, and specialized sports trainers (sneakers).
- **Wedding:** 
  - The Bride wears a white wedding dress. The Groom wears a formal tailored suit.
  - Guests: Elegant suits for men, and nice formal dresses for women (traditionally avoiding white so they don't overshadow the bride).
