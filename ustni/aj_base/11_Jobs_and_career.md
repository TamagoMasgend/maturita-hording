# 11 - Jobs and career

## 1. Elementary school
In the Czech Republic, education is compulsory for 9 years. Elementary school starts at the age of 6. Children learn basic subjects like the Czech language, mathematics, basic history, nature, and physical education. Around the 3rd or 4th grade, they start learning their first foreign language, which is almost always English. At the end of the 9th grade, pupils must decide their future path and pass entrance exams for secondary schools.

## 2. Secondary school
There are several types of secondary schools. 
- **Grammar schools (Gymnázium):** Provide general academic education preparing students for university. They do not give any specific professional qualification.
- **Secondary Technical Schools (Střední odborná škola):** Prepare students for specific professions like IT, economics, nursing, or engineering.
- **Vocational schools (Učiliště):** Teach practical trades like plumbing, carpentry, or hairdressing.
Students at grammar and technical schools finish their studies with a school-leaving exam called "Maturita," which is required to apply for universities (higher education).
